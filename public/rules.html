Quick rules — read before you play ⚡

🪪

Use your real name

Enter your actual first + last name when joining. Winners are announced by name — we need to know who you really are to hand over the prize. Nicknames or joke names will be disqualified.

🚀 How to Join

  1. Open the link shared by the host on your phone or browser.
  2. Enter your real name and pick an emoji avatar.
  3. The quiz starts automatically once the host kicks it off.
⚠️ Do not reload the page during the quiz — you will be disconnected and lose your place. Keep the tab open and active throughout.

📲 Answering

  • Tap A / B / C / D to lock in your answer.
  • You cannot change your answer once locked.
  • No answer before the timer hits zero = 0 points.
  • The host reveals the correct answer after everyone answers (or time runs out).

🏆 Scoring

EventPoints
✅ Correct answer+100 pts
❌ Wrong / no answer0 pts
🔥 3 correct in a row+200 pts bonus
🔥🔥 5 correct in a row+500 pts bonus
Speed doesn't affect your score — only accuracy does. Streaks reset if you answer wrong or miss a question.

⭐ Difficulty = Time Limit

Every correct answer is worth 100 pts regardless of difficulty. Harder questions just give you less time.
