BrainBlitz

Real-time quiz game — rules & how to play

🚀 How to Join a Game

  1. Go to brainblitz.app (or the host's URL) on your phone or any browser.
  2. Enter the 6-character room code shown on the host's screen.
  3. Pick a display name (max 24 characters, must be unique in the room).
  4. Choose an emoji avatar — it appears next to your name on the leaderboard.
  5. Wait in the lobby until the host starts the game.
💡 Tip: Keep the tab open throughout the game. If you get disconnected, the app will try to reconnect automatically.

🏆 Scoring

Event Points
✅ Correct answer +100 pts
❌ Wrong answer 0 pts
⏱️ No answer (timer runs out) 0 pts
🔥 3-correct streak bonus +200 pts (one-time at exactly 3)
🔥🔥 5-correct streak bonus +500 pts (one-time at exactly 5)
Example — 10 questions, all correct with a 5-streak:
Questions 1–5: 5 × 100 = 500 pts
+ 3-streak bonus at Q3: +200 pts
+ 5-streak bonus at Q5: +500 pts
Questions 6–10: 5 × 100 = 500 pts
Total: 1,700 pts

🔥 Streaks

  • A streak is a run of consecutive correct answers.
  • Bonus points are awarded at the 3rd and 5th correct answer in a row.
  • The streak resets to zero if you answer wrong or don't answer before the timer runs out.
  • Streaks continue across questions — you don't have to be fast, just correct.
  • When you hit a streak milestone, a 🔥 burst animation appears on your screen.

⭐ Difficulty Tiers

Difficulty controls how much time you have to answer — not the points. Every correct answer is worth 100 pts.

Tier Time to answer Points
⭐ Very Easy 8 seconds 100 pts
⭐⭐ Easy 12 seconds 100 pts
⭐⭐⭐ Medium 15 seconds 100 pts
⭐⭐⭐⭐ Hard 18 seconds 100 pts

📲 Answering Questions

  • Tap one of the A / B / C / D options to lock in your answer.
  • Once locked, you cannot change your answer.
  • A ⏳ badge confirms your answer was received.
  • If the timer reaches zero before you answer, you score 0 for that question.
  • After all players answer (or time runs out), the host reveals the correct answer.

🎮 Host Controls

Action What it does
Start Game Begins a 3-second countdown then launches Q1. Requires ≥1 player in the lobby.
Skip Question Ends the current timer early and reveals the answer immediately.
Next Question Advances to the next question after the reveal screen.
End Game Finishes the game immediately and shows the final leaderboard.

📋 Room Rules

  • Maximum 500 players per room.
  • Display names are case-insensitive unique — "Alice" and "alice" cannot both join.
  • You can only join a room while it is in the lobby (before the host starts).
  • If the host disconnects mid-game, the room closes and all players are notified.
  • Rooms with finished games stay open so the leaderboard display remains live.
  • Your answer history for this game is saved on your device for reference after the game ends.

📄 Question File Format (Hosts)

Upload a .docx file using one of these two formats:

Prose format:

Q: What is the capital of France? (12)
A) London
B) Berlin
C) Paris
D) Madrid
Answer: C

Table format:

Headers: Question | A | B | C | D | Answer
One row per question. Answer column = letter (A–D).

The number in parentheses sets the timer in seconds (8 = very easy, 12 = easy, 15 = medium, 18 = hard). Any other value defaults to the hard tier.
